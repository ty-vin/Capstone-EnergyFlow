Chakra 6: “Third eye”

Sanskrit & Meaning: Ajna - “perceiving”, “knowledge" Location: between the eyebrows Element: All the elements in pure form Sound or Mantra: KSAM or Ham Color: Indigo or bluish purple Message: “I am seeing and perceiving truthfully.” “I am filled with vision.” Balanced: Insight, imagination, intuition, illumination, inner peace Imbalanced: Rejection of spirituality, feeling stuck in life with out vision for future, poor clarity, fantasizing, headaches, vision problems Ways to Correct Imbalances: Visualize, meditate, open self to inner guide, use of indigo or bluish purple in surroundings
